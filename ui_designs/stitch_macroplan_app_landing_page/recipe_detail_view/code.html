Instructions

  1. 1

    Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice it into strips.

  2. 2

    In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing. Toss to coat evenly.

  3. 3

    Add the sliced grilled chicken, Parmesan cheese, and croutons (if using) to the bowl.

  4. 4

    Toss everything together gently and serve immediately.
